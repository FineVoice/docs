## Logo

The FineVoice logo represents the brand’s mission to provide high-quality, natural AI voices. The design combines a sleek, modern icon with clean, bold typography, reflecting innovation, clarity, and professionalism.

- **Icon:** The FineVoice icon features a sleek, abstract design formed by the letters F and V, seamlessly intertwined to create a flowing wave shape.
- **Wordmark:** The “FineVoice” text uses a modern, sans-serif font to convey clarity, simplicity, and approachability.

## **Usage Guidelines**

The FineVoice name and logos are trademarks of Fineshare Inc. You may freely use and redistribute our brand assets when referencing FineVoice. By using our brand assets, you agree that we own them and that any goodwill generated by your use benefits FineVoice.

## **Do**

- Use our brand assets freely in your projects, applications, and content
- Share our brand assets in blog posts, tutorials, documentation, and educational materials
- Follow the visual guidelines shown above (spacing, colors, sizing)
- Link to [FineVoice ](https://finevoice.ai/)when using our brand online

## **Don’t**

- Use our logo as part of your own product name or branding
- Imply partnership, sponsorship, or endorsement without permission
- Feature our logo more prominently than your own brand
